Why jeeya.socials

I work exclusively inside mental health.

I created jeeya.socials for psychologists, clinical psychologists, counselling psychologists, counsellors, therapists, psychotherapists, relationship counsellors, child psychologists, marriage and family therapists, mental health clinics, private practices and solo practitioners.

i

Mental health is not marketed like everything else.

Most agencies treat therapists the same way they treat restaurants, clothing brands or real estate listings. The work is different here. Trust is different. Ethics are different. Client confidentiality matters before any campaign idea does.

ii

The client journey is quieter, slower and more human.

People do not choose a psychologist or counsellor because a trend caught their attention. They look for safety, clarity, credibility and a sense that you understand the problem they are carrying.

iii

Generic marketing fails because it ignores the responsibility.

I work only in this profession, so every message, visual system, landing page and content plan is built around trust, confidentiality, Islamic values and ethical visibility.

"If you help people heal, your marketing should protect the dignity of that work."

Is social media marketing actually halal?
+
Yes, when done carefully. I do not use music in videos, manipulative persuasion tactics, fake urgency or content that conflicts with Islamic sensibilities. My framework is built on honesty, value and authentic positioning.
Will this work for my specific niche?
+
I work exclusively with mental health professionals, so the process already accounts for ethical constraints, professional boundaries, careful language and the decision journey of a therapy client.
How much of my time does this actually take?
+
Usually 20 to 30 minutes a month. I send content for approval, you review it, request changes if needed, and approve. Strategy, design, writing and scheduling are handled for you.
Do I have to appear on camera?
+
No, not unless you want to. I can build a content strategy that works without face-to-camera video and still represents you well.
I'm not established yet — is this too early?
+
Starting early can be an advantage. I can help you build positioning and visual consistency before years of scattered posting make the brand harder to refine.
What if I'm not happy with the content?
+
Nothing goes live without your approval. If something does not feel right, I revise it. Your voice, your values, your brand. I execute, you approve.
What does it cost?
+
Pricing depends on what you actually need, not a generic bundle. The free audit is the right first step because I can see where you are, where you want to go, and what would move the needle first.
